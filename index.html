Tiered Intervention Plan

Structured support framework aligned with MOE SPN21 & RTG 2026 strategy

🔴 TIER 1 — Intensive Support
~26
Who: Students with Grade E in Maths + 1 other subject
Frequency: Daily pull-out sessions (Term 1–3)
AI Tools: Adaptive drill apps, AI flashcards, text-to-speech for dyslexia support
Monitoring: Weekly grade check via this dashboard
KPI: Move from E → D by SBA1, D → C by Mock
🟡 TIER 2 — Targeted Intervention
~18
Who: Grade D–E in 1–2 subjects, not qualifying for Tier 1
Frequency: 3× weekly small-group sessions
AI Tools: AI quiz generators (Quizizz), grammar checkers, concept mapping AI
Monitoring: Biweekly progress review by subject teacher
KPI: Move from D → C by SBA2, maintain C → B by PSR
🟢 TIER 3 — Enrichment & Leadership
~13
Who: Consistent Grade A–B performers across subjects
Focus: 5A nurture programme, peer tutor training
AI Tools: AI essay enhancement, advanced problem sets, research AI tools
Monitoring: Monthly enrichment review
KPI: Minimum 3 students achieve 5A in PSR 2026
